Common questions

Am I required to use Innovate Realty or Honest Escrow?
No. Use of any introduced provider is completely optional. You are free to choose your own real estate broker, escrow company, title company, lender, and other service providers.
Does opting in affect my CSL loan?
No. Use of any introduced provider is not required as a condition of obtaining, maintaining, or paying off a CSL loan. Requesting information has no effect on your loan terms or servicing.
What does it cost to request information?
Nothing. Requesting information is free. If you later choose to work with any provider, applicable fees are subject to eligibility, transaction details, party agreement, written disclosure, and escrow instructions.
What happens after I submit the form?
A team member reaches out within 24 hours with the information you selected. If you chose “follow up later,” we'll simply check back closer to your timeline.
