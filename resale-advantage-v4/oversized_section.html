Program Benefits

What you can request through the program

Eligible CSL borrowers can ask for information about any of the following. Everything is optional — pick what's useful to you.

Exclusive 1% listing fee

A pre-negotiated 1% listing commission through Innovate Realty — versus the typical 2.5–3% — with InnoLaunch marketing included when you list with an Innovate agent.

Listing marketing support

InnoLaunch marketing support to help put your property in front of more buyers — Innovate can even begin marketing before construction is finished, so you reach the market sooner. Included when you list with an Innovate Realty agent. See samples below ↓

Southern California agent network

Access to Innovate's Southern California agent network for your listing.

An escrow option that knows CSL

Honest Escrow is familiar with CSL payoff and closing requirements. For CSL borrowers, seller‑side escrow fees are reduced — as low as $500 per seller; buyer‑side fees remain standard.*

Earlier resale planning

Start planning the resale before your project is fully complete, so listing, escrow, and payoff line up instead of stacking up.

One coordinated process

A more coordinated process between you, your lender, your agent, and the escrow team — designed to reduce friction at closing.

*Pending escrow-law / legal review: an advertised escrow fee reduction must be disclosed in writing and reflected in the escrow instructions.
